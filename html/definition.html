Standard Dictionary Definition

unicorn /ˈjuːnɪkɔːn/ noun 1. a mythical animal typically represented as a horse with a single straight horn projecting from its forehead. 2. something that is highly desirable but difficult to find or obtain. "an album like this is something of a unicorn"
